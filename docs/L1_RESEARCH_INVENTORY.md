# L1 Research Inventory

This public repository keeps L1, appchain, proof-system, bridge, and native-state research behind explicit gates.

## Public Boundary

- The public repo may describe research directions, but it must not expose private research worktree paths, private source archives, operator host details, or raw copied research prompts.
- Legacy research corpora are reference material only. They are not production code, not deployment evidence, and not approval for a token, validator network, bridge, appchain, or mainnet launch.
- Any concept promoted from research into FlowMemory must enter through a reviewed decision record, a narrow implementation issue, tests, and updated public claim boundaries.

## Current Near-Term Scope

The active public scope remains FlowMemory V0 and the public-agent / Agent Bonds local-test stack:

- compact memory roots and receipts;
- FlowPulse event boundaries;
- verifier reports and replayable state transitions;
- fixture-backed indexer, verifier, control-plane, dashboard, SDK, and CLI paths;
- local/test public-agent and bounded Agent Bonds flows.

## Not Approved From Research Alone

Research notes do not approve:

- production L1 or appchain implementation;
- validator, sequencer, bridge, or token launch plans;
- production proof-system claims;
- imported legacy code without review;
- public claims that exceed the tested local/canary evidence in this repository.

## Promotion Rule

When research becomes implementation, write a decision record that names:

1. the exact FlowMemory object or contract affected;
2. the accepted claim boundary;
3. the rejected alternatives;
4. the tests and fixtures that prove the behavior;
5. the deployment or production claims that remain blocked.
